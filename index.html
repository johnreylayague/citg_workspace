About Us

The Philippine's first and only urban attraction of its kind! Home of Cebu's iconic Sky Walk & Edge Coaster! Located at the top of Crown Regency Hotel & Towers. It's the ultimate high!

Sky-High Standards

Soar above the city with confidence—every element of the Sky Walk is engineered for thrill and stability. From the panoramic glass flooring to the precision-built walkways, quality meets adrenaline at 127 meters up.

Certified Safety First

Equipped with international-grade harnesses and lifelines, every step on the Sky Walk is guided by safety professionals and secured by upgraded gear. Walk the edge knowing you're protected from start to finish.
